About Us

Welcome to Side Hustle, a cozy wine bar in Seattle, WA specializing in low-intervention wines made in Washington State. Join us to explore our unique selection and share our passion for local, sustainable winemaking.

Our Wine Selection

Discover our curated selection of low-intervention wines. From bold reds to refreshing whites, each bottle tells the story of Washington's rich winemaking tradition and the dedicated artisans behind them.
